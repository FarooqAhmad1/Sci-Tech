About Us

We are an international holding company at the intersection of science, technology, health, and wellness. We use human intelligence to advance global health, societal wellbeing, and human welfare.

We are centered in our quest to achieve balance. We strike an equilibrium between automation and science, so that, by pushing the boundaries of both, we create products and services that advance health and humanity around the world.

We have a global presence spanning 4 continents across 30 countries.
